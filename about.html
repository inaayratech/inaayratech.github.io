About Us

Simple Solutions for Everyday Life

Inaayra Tech is a small software company focused on creating practical applications that make daily tasks easier. We believe that good software doesn't need to be complicated – it just needs to work well and serve its purpose.

Our Approach

As an independent developer, I started Inaayra Tech with a straightforward goal: build apps that I would want to use myself. No unnecessary features, no confusing interfaces – just clean, functional tools that help you get things done.

What We Do

We develop applications for macOS and other platforms, focusing on productivity and utility. Each app we create follows the same principle: solve a specific problem well, without adding unnecessary complexity. Whether it's task management, note-taking, or any other daily activity, our tools are designed to integrate seamlessly into your workflow.

Why Inaayra Tech?

In a world of feature-heavy software, we choose to go the other direction. We focus on doing a few things really well rather than trying to do everything. Our apps are designed to be helpful companions in your daily routine, not another source of complexity.

Looking Forward

While we're a small operation, we're committed to maintaining and improving our applications based on user feedback. We're here to build tools that genuinely help people, one simple app at a time.
