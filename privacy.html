Introduction

Inaayra Tech LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices for all applications developed and distributed by Inaayra Tech.

1. Information Collection

1.1 No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, process, or transmit any personal information from our applications. This includes:

  • No user analytics or usage statistics
  • No crash reports or diagnostic data
  • No personal identifiers or device information
  • No location data or network information

1.2 Local Storage Only

All data created within our applications remains exclusively on your device:

  • Application content and user-generated data
  • Preferences and settings
  • Application state and cache

2. Data Security

Since we do not collect or have access to your data, the security of your information depends entirely on your device's security measures. We recommend keeping your device and operating system updated.

3. Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs that would have access to your data.

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect information from anyone, including children. All data remains locally stored on the user's device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of our applications after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

6. Your Rights

Since we don't collect any data, there is no personal information for you to access, correct, or delete from our servers. All your data is under your complete control on your device.
