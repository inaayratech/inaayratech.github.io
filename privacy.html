Introduction

Inaayra Tech LLC ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This page outlines our company-wide privacy principles. Each of our applications has its own Privacy Policy with specific details about data handling for that app.

1. Per-App Privacy Policies

Each application published by Inaayra Tech maintains its own Privacy Policy tailored to that app's specific features and data practices. Please refer to the relevant app's Privacy Policy for detailed information:

  • Battle Within — Privacy Policy

If an app-specific Privacy Policy conflicts with this company-level policy, the app-specific policy takes precedence for that application.

2. Company-Wide Privacy Principles

Our apps are designed with privacy in mind. Inaayra Tech follows these core principles:

  • Privacy by design: We build our apps to minimize data collection and keep your information under your control.
  • No data sales: We never sell or monetize your personal information.
  • Transparency: Each app's Privacy Policy clearly discloses what data is collected, how it is used, and what third-party services are involved.
  • Your control: You can delete your data at any time by removing the app from your device.

3. iCloud Sync

Some of our applications may use Apple's iCloud service to sync data across your devices. When iCloud sync is available:

  • Your data is stored in your personal iCloud account, not on our servers
  • Inaayra Tech does not have access to your iCloud data
  • Data syncing is governed by Apple's iCloud Terms of Service and Privacy Policy
  • You can disable iCloud sync at any time through your device settings

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect information from anyone, including children. All data remains locally stored on the user's device.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Effective Date" above. Continued use of our applications after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
