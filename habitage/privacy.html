Habitage Privacy Terms

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 27, 2026 · Version 1.0

1. Overview

Habitage ("we", "our", or "the app") is a habit tracking application developed by Inaayra Tech LLC. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Data We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. Habitage does not require an account, email address, or any personal information to use.

2.1 Habit Data

All habit data (habits, completions, streaks, achievements) is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. This data never leaves your device except through iCloud sync (see Section 3).

2.2 No Analytics or Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising frameworks. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral information.

3. iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, Habitage can sync your habit data across your Apple devices using Apple's CloudKit framework. This data is:

  • Stored in your private iCloud container
  • Encrypted in transit and at rest by Apple
  • Accessible only by you through your Apple ID
  • Subject to Apple's Privacy Policy

We cannot access, read, or modify your iCloud data.

4. Subscriptions

Habitage offers subscription plans managed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not process, store, or have access to your payment information. All billing is handled by Apple under their terms of service.

5. Notifications

Habitage may send local notifications for habit reminders if you enable them. These notifications are generated and scheduled entirely on your device. No notification data is sent to any server.

6. Children's Privacy

Habitage does not knowingly collect data from children under 13. Since we collect no personal data from any user, the app is safe for users of all ages.

7. Third-Party Services

Habitage does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs beyond Apple's native frameworks (SwiftData, CloudKit, StoreKit, UserNotifications).

8. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual habits within the app
  • Uninstalling the app (removes all local data)
  • Managing iCloud storage through iOS Settings

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected with an updated effective date at the top of this page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at apps@inaayratech.com.
